About Me

I'm an Electrical Engineering junior at UC Irvine, transferring from Saddleback College where I earned associate's degrees in Physics and Mathematics. My work centers on robotics — designing the electronics and embedded systems that make machines move, sense, and operate reliably in demanding environments.

I contributed to a Mars rover that placed in the top 20% of 100+ teams at the University Rover Challenge for two years, designing custom PCBs and programming microcontrollers along the way. I currently manage undergraduate robotics teams at UCI's Zotbotics club, perform energy assessments at UCI SMART ITAC, and work as an electronics technician at SEP Electronics.

Outside of robotics, I explore radio astronomy, PCB design, and software — drawn to any project that sits at the edge of what's been done before.
